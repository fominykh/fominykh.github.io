    About

    Hi! I am a researcher and project manager working in the area of Technology-Enhanced Learning. My main focus is on teaching at the university level, collaborative learning, and 3D simulations, but I work with other target groups and technologies too.

    One of my main academic achievemtns has been to co-author the idea, get funded and co-lead the Research-and-Innovation project WEKIT, funded by EU under Horizon 2020 programme. The video presents the industrial trials of the WEKIT platform in Healthcare and Space conducted in 2018-2019.
